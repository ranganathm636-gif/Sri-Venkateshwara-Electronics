FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting or calling us.

What types of electronics do you repair?
We repair all types of TVs (LED, Smart, LCD, CRT/old models), kitchen appliances (mixers, grinders, mixies), audio systems, amplifiers, and most household electronics. Call us if unsure — we'll let you know immediately.
Is the diagnostic check really free?
Yes, the initial diagnostic check is just ₹100 — one of the lowest in Madhugiri. We identify the exact fault and give you a repair estimate BEFORE starting any work. No surprises.
Do you provide a warranty on repairs?
Yes! We provide a service warranty on all repairs. The duration depends on the type of repair and parts used. We use only genuine spare parts to ensure longer-lasting repairs.
How long does a repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 1–3 working days. Simple faults (like a mixer repair or minor TV issue) can often be done the same day. We'll give you a realistic timeline after the diagnostic.
What brands do you service?
All major brands: Sony, Samsung, LG, Philips, Onida, Videocon, Panasonic, Bajaj, Preethi, Prestige, and more. Both Indian and international brands across all product categories.
Do you sell used electronics? Are they reliable?
Yes! We sell quality-tested used TVs and appliances. Every item is thoroughly inspected, tested, and any faults repaired before we put it up for sale. You get an honest condition report and a short warranty.
